About the data fields:

- ZCTA5: zipcode
- LANDSQMT: land square meters
- WATERSQMT: water square meters
- LANDSQMI: land square miles
- WATERSQMI: water square miles
- POPULATION: population
- HSGUNITS: housing units
- INTPTLAT: latitude
- INTPTLON: longitude
